♫Game Play♫

Phantom of The Opera is a point and click styled game for the Nintendo Switch but can also be played on PC and mobile devices. To tell the story of the Phantom of The Opera, the game changes characters, the most frequent one being Christine. There are minigames and puzzles to solve in order to open secret doors and items. The game provides an amazing OST and music from famous operas like “Ah, je ris de me voir” from Faust.

For the purpose of this study, I will focus on the music aspects of the game and how it affects the gameplay.

♫Music♫

♪Don Juan Triumphant♪

Similarly to the original novel, Phantom of the Opera by MazM includes the opera Erik wrote, Don Juan Triumphant. After Christine gets kidnapped by Erik, she converses with him about the music sheets on his pipe organ and asks him to play for her. Erik tells her that she can't listen to his opera because it will overpower any human. However, this song serves as the title screen music, which is a little paradoxal.
